About TEE TVET COLLEGE

,We are committed to excellence in the delivery of our industry-aligned learning programme offerings. Thus, we ensure that our course curriculum content remains relevant and aligned with local and global standards and best practices. Our facilitation team is subject matter experts with extensive industry-related experience and in our pursuit of continuous improvement, we have implemented a total quality management system. Our methodology is focused on working with our retail and manufacturing partners. Together we encourage the implementation of world-class manufacturing standards and processes to strengthen the capacity of our local supply chain and meet the goals set out in the R-CTFL Masterplan 2030. A major focus area is empowering learners, be it workers in the sector or unemployed youth, by equipping them with relevant workplace knowledge and technical skills, and increasing their employability and growth in the formal and informal sectors. Our current involvement in rural and township social development initiatives demonstrates our commitment to empowering disadvantaged communities provision of innovative skills development interventions, geared to stimulate the establishment of small community-based businesses. TEE TVET (Pty) Ltd values people and we hold excellence, integrity and respect to the highest standard. Our promise is to do the right thing and to do things right.!
